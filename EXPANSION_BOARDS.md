# Expansion boards

## Board layout

Six pins are connected to 14pin PIC16F1 DIP.

```
 1   2   3   4
 5V GND RA5 RA4
 o   o   o   o
 .   .   .   .
 .   .   .   .
 .   .   .   .
 .   .   .   .
 .   .   .   .
 .   .   .   .
 o   o   o   o
 RA3 RC5 RC4 RC3
 4   3   2   1
```
